> feature_map

Built to make food logging feel calm, fast, and quietly addictive.

01 / input

Source-backed search, with AI when needed

Use fast food search first, then fall back to AI-assisted queries when product names get messy or incomplete.

02 / capture

Barcode scan straight into the log

Point, scan, confirm, and keep moving. Logging should feel closer to terminal shortcuts than form filling.

03 / dashboard

Calories and macros in one readable stack

See calories, protein, carbs, and fat together with a floating summary instead of hunting across screens.

04 / sync

Apple Health as the nutrition backbone

Import dietary data, write meals back, and keep goals grounded in the health data you already trust.

05 / glance

Widgets that keep the day visible

Macros should not disappear when the app closes. Widgets keep the current state one swipe away.

06 / texture

Terminal mood, ocean palette

Monospaced rhythm, dark water gradients, phosphor-like accents, and a calmer alternative to generic health UI.
